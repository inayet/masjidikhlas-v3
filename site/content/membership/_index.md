# Become a Member

Join Masjid Ikhlas as an official member and participate in the decision-making process of our community.

## Membership Benefits

- Voting rights in community decisions
- Priority access to programs and events
- Discount on educational programs
- Regular updates on community initiatives

Please visit the masjid during office hours or contact us for membership information.
