# Donate

## Support Test Mosque

Test Mosque relies entirely on the generous support of our community to continue providing essential services to the Metropolitan Denver North Muslim community. Your contributions help us maintain our facilities, offer educational programs, and serve those in need.

## Ways to Give

### Online Donations
Make a secure online donation using credit/debit cards or bank transfer.

**Monthly Giving Options:**
- **$25/month** - Supports one child's Islamic education
- **$50/month** - Helps maintain our facilities  
- **$100/month** - Sponsors a community event
- **$250/month** - Supports our outreach programs

### Zakat and Sadaqah
Fulfill your religious obligations through Test Mosque.

**Zakat Eligible Programs:**
- Educational scholarships for needy students
- Emergency financial assistance
- Food pantry and social services
- New Muslim support services

### Special Funds

#### Masjid Expansion Fundraising
Help us expand our facilities to better serve our growing community.

#### Zakat al-Fitr & Ramadan Fundraising
Support our Ramadan programs and Zakat al-Fitr distribution.
