# Outreach Department

Test Mosque's outreach team focuses on teaching about Islam to the Greater Denver community while hosting different events. Our Masjid open house is every 1st Saturday of the month from 2:30 - 5 pm. It is free and open to the public.

If you have any questions regarding Islam, want to schedule a mosque visit or request a speaker, please contact us at **outreach@CO** and we will be happy to talk to you.

## Programs & Services

### About Islam Presentations
Educational presentations about Islam for schools, organizations, and community groups. Topics include:
- Basic beliefs and practices
- Islamic traditions and culture
- Common misconceptions about Islam
- Women in Islam
- Islamic contributions to civilization

### Mosque Tours
Guided tours of Test Mosque facility for:
- School groups
- Interfaith organizations
- Community groups
- New visitors
- Anyone interested in learning about Islam

### Speaker Requests
Knowledgeable speakers available for:
- Interfaith dialogues
- Community events
- Educational institutions
- Media inquiries
- Workplace diversity training

### Open House
**Every 1st Saturday of the month, 2:30 PM - 5:00 PM**
- Free and open to the public
- Refreshments provided
- Q&A sessions
- Meet the community

### Volunteer Opportunities
Join our outreach team to help with:
- Event organization
- Community education
- Interfaith activities
- Mosque tours
