# Volunteer with Masjid Ikhlas

Lend your time and skills to support our various programs and community initiatives.

## Volunteer Opportunities

### Education Programs
- Sunday School teachers and assistants
- Quran class helpers
- Youth program mentors

### Community Services
- Food pantry volunteers
- Event setup and cleanup
- Welcome committee members

### Administrative Support
- Office assistance
- Social media management
- Newsletter preparation

### Maintenance & Operations
- Facility maintenance
- Groundskeeping
- Security team

## How to Get Started

1. **Fill out the volunteer form** at the masjid office
2. **Attend orientation** (held monthly)
3. **Choose your area** of interest
4. **Complete background check** (for positions working with children)

## Volunteer Requirements

- Commitment of at least 4 hours per month
- Regular attendance and punctuality
- Positive attitude and teamwork
- Respect for Islamic values and community guidelines

## Benefits of Volunteering

- Gain valuable experience
- Build community connections
- Develop new skills
- Make a meaningful impact
- Receive letters of recommendation
