# About Us

## Metropolitan Denver North Islamic Center

Metropolitan Denver North Islamic Center was legally established in March 1999. The Center is a Non-Profit, Educational, Religious and Humanitarian Organization.

Masjid Al-Ikhlas is committed to the Islamic way of life based on Qur'an and life example of Prophet Muhammad. We serve as a center for religious development, social, educational, economic, and cultural enrichment. We believe in Oneness of God (Allah) and oneness of humanity. We are committed to promoting excellence in community life and human dignity throughout our neighborhoods, city, country, and global community.

## Our Mission

To provide a peaceful place for worship, Islamic education, and community building while fostering understanding and harmony among all people, based on the principles of **"Faith in Allah, Simple Living, Hard Work & High Thinking."**

## Our Values

### Faith & Worship
We provide a clean, peaceful environment for daily prayers, Friday Jumu'ah, and special Ramadan prayers following the Qur'an and Sunnah.

### Education
We offer comprehensive Islamic education through Ikhlas Academy and IQRA Academy for children and adults, including Quranic studies, Arabic language, and Islamic principles.

### Community Service
Our mosque is actively involved in serving the broader community through outreach programs, interfaith dialogues, and humanitarian services.

### Unity & Diversity
We welcome Muslims from all backgrounds and traditions, fostering an environment of mutual respect and understanding according to Islamic teachings.
