# About Us

## Metropolitan Denver North Islamic Center

Metropolitan Denver North Islamic Center was legally established in March 1999. The Center is a Non-Profit, Educational, Religious and Humanitarian Organization.

Masjid Al-Ikhlas is committed to Islamic way of life based on Qur'an and life example of Prophet Muhammad. We serve as a center for religious development, social, educational, economic, and cultural enrichment. We believe in Oneness of God (Allah) and oneness of humanity.

## Our Mission

To provide a peaceful place for worship, Islamic education, and community building while fostering understanding and harmony among all people.

## Our Values

### Faith & Worship
We provide a clean, peaceful environment for daily prayers, Friday Jumu'ah, and special Ramadan prayers.

### Education
We offer comprehensive Islamic education through Ikhlas Academy and IQRA Academy for children and adults.

### Community Service
Our mosque is actively involved in serving the broader community through outreach programs and interfaith dialogues.
