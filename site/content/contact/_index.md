## Visit Us

### Parking
Free parking is available on-site and on surrounding streets.

### Accessibility
Our mosque is wheelchair accessible with ramp entrance and accessible facilities.

### First Time Visitors
If you're visiting for the first time, we recommend:
- Arrive 15 minutes before prayer time
- Dress modestly (loose-fitting clothing)
- Bring prayer rug (optional - we provide them)
- Remove shoes before entering prayer area

### Guided Tours
We offer guided tours of our facility for:
- School groups
- Interfaith organizations
- Community groups
- New visitors

To schedule a tour, please contact us at least one week in advance.
