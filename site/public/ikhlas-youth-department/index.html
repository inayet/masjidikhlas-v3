Ikhlas Youth Department

Ikhlas Youth Department

The Ikhlas Youth Department is dedicated to providing a positive Islamic environment for Muslim youth in Metropolitan Denver North. We offer programs that nurture faith, build character, and develop leadership skills while creating lasting friendships and community bonds.

Programs

Girls Youth Program

A comprehensive program designed specifically for Muslim sisters ages 12-18.

Activities Include:

  • Islamic education and Quran studies
  • Leadership development workshops
  • Community service projects
  • Social events and gatherings
  • Mentorship opportunities
  • Sports and recreational activities

Meeting Times:

  • Fridays: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
  • Special events throughout the month

Boys Youth Program

Engaging programs for Muslim brothers ages 12-18 focused on Islamic development and character building.

Activities Include:

  • Islamic studies and halaqas
  • Sports leagues and tournaments
  • Leadership training
  • Community service initiatives
  • Educational workshops
  • Outdoor activities and camps

Meeting Times:

  • Fridays: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
  • Weekly sports activities
  • Monthly special events

Special Events

Youth Camps

Summer Islamic Camp

  • Islamic education and activities
  • Team building and leadership
  • Outdoor adventures

Leadership Workshops

Monthly workshops focusing on:

  • Islamic leadership principles
  • Public speaking skills

  • Mentorship training

Community Service

Regular opportunities for youth to:

  • Volunteer at local charities
  • Organize community projects
  • Help those in need
  • Develop civic responsibility

Educational Focus

Islamic Studies

  • Quran recitation and memorization
  • Islamic beliefs and practices
  • Life of Prophet Muhammad (Peace be upon him)
  • Islamic history and civilization
  • Contemporary Islamic issues

Character Development

  • Islamic morals and values
  • Leadership skills
  • Teamwork and cooperation
  • Respect and responsibility
  • Community service

Registration

How to Join

  1. Complete Registration Form available at masjid office
  2. Parental Consent required for all participants
  3. Annual Fee: $50 per youth (scholarships available)
  4. Attend Orientation first Friday of each month

Required Documents

  • Completed registration form
  • Parental consent form
  • Emergency contact information
  • Medical information form

Volunteer Opportunities

  • Teaching Islamic classes
  • Coaching sports teams
  • Chaperoning field trips
