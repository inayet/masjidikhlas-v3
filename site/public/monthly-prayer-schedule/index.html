Prayer Time Calculation Method

We use the Islamic Society of North America (ISNA) calculation method adjusted for the Denver, Colorado coordinates.

Notes

  • Fajr and Isha times are calculated using the standard method
  • Asr time is calculated using the Hanafi school of thought
  • Times are adjusted monthly to account for seasonal changes
  • During Ramadan, special schedules will be posted
