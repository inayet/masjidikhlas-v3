Events & Programs

Events & Programs

Regular Weekly Events

Friday Jumu’ah

Every Friday at 1:00 PM Join us for the weekly congregational prayer and khutbah (sermon). The sermon covers topics relevant to contemporary Muslim life and is delivered in English.

Family Night

Every Saturday after Maghrib prayer Potluck dinner followed by Islamic lecture and family activities. All families are welcome to join.

Sisters’ Circle

Every Wednesday, 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM A gathering for sisters to learn, share, and support each other in their spiritual journey.

Monthly Events

Community Iftar

During Ramadan, daily at sunset Join us for breaking fast together during the blessed month of Ramadan. All community members are welcome.

Interfaith Dialogue

First Sunday of each month, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM Open discussions with people of different faiths to promote understanding and cooperation.

Youth Halaqa

Second Friday of each month, 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM Educational and social gathering for youth ages 13-25.

Special Events

Eid Celebrations

Eid al-Fitr and Eid al-Adha Join us for festive Eid prayers followed by community celebrations, food, and activities for all ages.

Annual Fundraising Gala

Fall season Our major fundraising event to support the mosque’s operations and community programs.

Health & Wellness Fair

Spring season Free health screenings, consultations with healthcare professionals, and wellness workshops.

Back to School Drive

August Collection and distribution of school supplies for families in need.

Winter Coat Drive

November - December Collection and distribution of warm clothing for those in need.

Educational Workshops

Financial Planning Workshop

Quarterly Islamic perspective on personal finance, budgeting, and investment.

Parenting Seminar

Bi-monthly Raising children with Islamic values in the modern world.

Marriage Enrichment Workshop

Quarterly Strengthening marital relationships through Islamic principles.
